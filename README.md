# TaxiAnalisis

This project was conducted as a data scientist for Zuber, a new ride-sharing company launching in Chicago. The primary objective was to inform the launch strategy by finding patterns in ride-share data, understanding passenger preferences, and analyzing the impact of external factors on trip metrics.
The analysis uses statistical testing and exploratory data visualization to provide evidence-based insights for operational planning and competitive positioning in the Chicago market.

Visualizations were used to understand passenger preferences and market dynamics:

1. Top Passenger Destinations: Identified the Loop, River North, and Streeterville as the top dropoff locations by average trips, critical for determining fleet positioning and driver incentives.

2. Competitive Landscape: Analyzed cab company trip volume, showing Flash Cab and Taxi Affiliation Services as the market leaders. This informs Zuber's pricing and marketing strategy.

3. Duration Distribution: Histograms illustrated the difference in trip duration distributions between different conditions, providing visual context for the statistical findings.
